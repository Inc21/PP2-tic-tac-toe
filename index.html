The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. You play on a three by three game board. The first player is known as Player X and the second is Player O. Players alternate placing X's and O's on the game board until either opponent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. Player X always goes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat game.
